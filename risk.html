    Model & assumptions. Each trade is independent: a loss at your stop, a minimum-lock win (price stalls right after your trail arms at 1.25R, leaving the stop half-a-stop behind), or a runner win, with the probabilities you set. P&L is tracked from $0. The trailing limit follows your end-of-day peak and locks at break-even once you're up the drawdown amount (Topstep's end-of-day trailing MLL). The daily-loss limit stops the day; the consistency rule blocks a "pass" until your best day is ≤ 50% of total profit. ~2,500 simulations per update (20,000 on Solve), capped at 250 days. This models a mechanical, fixed-edge strategy — real markets have streaks, regime shifts, slippage, and execution error that make outcomes worse than a clean i.i.d. model. Treat it as a planning tool, not a promise. Specs verified vs CME, NinjaTrader, and Topstep's 2026 help-center / rules pages. Not financial advice.
